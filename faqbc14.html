    Account Questions

    Can I change my account data?
    Yes, from your user panel you can change your full name, password, change 2 Factor Authentication settings, and change wallets / withdrawal addresses.
    Any changes to your account require confirmation with a verification code sent to your email. Changes will be made immediately after entering the code.
    If you want to make other unlisted changes or correct a mistake please contact our support team, which is available 24/7.
    What to do if I forget my account password?
    Under the login window you will find the option "Forgot password". Enter your E-Mail or username in the form. After a while, you will receive an activation link to your e-mail address.
    When you do this, you will receive another email with your username and temporary password. After logging in, we recommend that you change the password in the "Edit Account" section to strong ones containing upper / lower case letters, numbers / or special characters.
    Can I Register more than one account?
    Yes, you can register more than one account if you need it for your own use. Your household members can also use one IP address serving different accounts. Please use different usernames and different e-mail addresses.
    Very important: do not register accounts in the referral program, only to get a commission on your own deposits. Accounts in the structure belonging to one person must have an active deposit. Using the referral program in a manner inconsistent with its intended use may result in the blocking of all accounts.
    Can I use phantomstrade .com on mobile devices?
    Yes, our website is fully responsive and adapted to all kinds of devices. You can perform all operations using your smartphone or tablet, and read all posted content.

    Questions about Deposits

    Can I own more than one deposit?
    Yes of course. You can have an unlimited number of deposits. You can make multiple deposits in different investment plans and using different payment methods. Each deposit is treated as a separate investment plan with a separate duration.
    How long after the transfer will the deposit be activated?
    Deposits in cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin ... etc are added after the third confirmation from the Blockchain network. Depending on the cryptocurrency and the transfer fee, it may take up to 1 hour.
    Can I cancel my Deposit and withdraw funds?
    After the first 24 hours of making a deposit, you can cancel it and withdraw funds. The amount you cancel will be charged a fee of 10%. Once your deposit is canceled, the funds will be returned to your balance from where you can withdraw. Deposits are returned free of charge upon completion of the investment plan (after 30, 40 or 50 days depending on the plan).
